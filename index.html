Project Timeline (May - December 2025): Strategic Analysis

This analysis segments the completed actions and projects from the 2025 timeline into the categories of Branding, Events, Internal Communications, and External Communications, providing a clear view of the strategic focus in each area.

Branding & Infrastructure

May Brandbook Update (Phase 1)

Start of the brand guide update cycle, essential for visual and verbal consistency.

June IT Infrastructure (Brand)

Creation of the brand-comms.cleveradvertising.com subdomain completed (Ticket ITS-15455), establishing a dedicated platform.

September Creation of Clever Canva Master Template

Standardization of design assets for use across the organization, ensuring agility and brand alignment.

Events & Presence

June SBC Summit Design Definition

Approval of the booth design ("V" structure) and delivery of the revised proposal, aligning the physical presence with the brand code.

July SBC Summit Budget and Planning

Approval of the final booth budget, definition of payment schedule, and finalization of the communication package (video, artwork, LinkedIn editorial calendar).

September Event Execution: SBC Summit Lisbon

Completion of VIP guest and partner registration, marking the peak of event presence.

November Malta Office Inauguration

Official opening event for the new space in Malta, a milestone in the company's physical expansion.

Internal & Structural Communications

June Management Meeting

Compilation of the agenda, preliminary summary, and organization of the in-person/online meeting finalized.

August Migration of Institutional Channel to Teams

Structural change in communication, centralizing information and improving internal accessibility.

October Meal Process Update

Definition and communication of the new meal request flow via Teams for the offices.

November Comms Portal Relaunch & CleverBoard

Launch of the updated Internal Communications Portal and start of CleverBoard tool testing.

December Q1 Bull Run & Workshop

Structuring of the Bull Run for the following Q1 and conducting the Github Workshop for the People and Comms team.

External & ESG Communications

June ESG Talk Launch (Episode 1)

Debut and external (and internal) communication of the series on sustainability and governance, raising the brand's ESG presence and commitment.

July Comms Planning (LinkedIn)

Inclusion of a dedicated LinkedIn editorial calendar in the SBC Summit package, reinforcing external communication of event presence.
